Speaker Details

Mukesh Jain

Mukesh Jain is an Educationist, Author, Coach and Techno-Biz Leader with 23+ years of experience working with names like Bill Gates and Satya Nadella and Mukesh Ambani. He is Currently the Vice President and Head of Insights, AI & Data Technology at Capgemini, building large scale Product Innovation by leveraging Data, Insights and AI for Global Fortune500 Companies. An ex Vice President of Jio, he is part of the Advisory committee and a teacher at Welingkar Institute of Management WeSchool, MGM, etc. He is the recipient of multiple awards, including Microsoft’s most prestigious “Gold Star”, awarded 3 years in a row, the Asia-Pacific Leadership Award and a lot more.

Sabyasachi Mukhopadhyay

Sabyasachi Mukhopadhyay is a Google Developer Expert in Machine Learning, but that's not where his list of achievements ends. Mukhopadhyay is an established author, researcher and a TEDx speaker. The co-founder of Twelit, a centre for AI-driven healthcare solutions, he is an expert in his field of work. We look forward to having him with us at the ML Accelerator Summit, 2019.

Shivam Abhilash

Shivam is currently working in the Big Data team of ZS associates. An expert in the field of Machine Learning, he has worked on a large number of technical and professional projects. He has given talks as Guest Speaker in more than 25 conferences on Machine Learning and its applications and the various projects that he has been working on. He is also one of the Richard E Merwin Scholars from the IEEE Computer Society. We've had the honour of working with him and having him as our guide in our previous event, Think AI. His experience in the field and the power-packed interactive sessions are extremely enriching.

Avelo Roy

Avelo Roy is a passionate tech entrepreneur, specialising in idea-to-market life cycles, venture capital and entrepreneurship education. A distinguished public speaker and motivator, he is well-known to instill among the Indian youth, a passion for entrepreneurship. Over the past ten years, he has personally mentored 1.5k+ young men and women all over the world.
