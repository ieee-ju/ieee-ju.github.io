About Us

IEEE is the world's largest technical professional organization that is working constantly towards the advancement of technology and to promote research, mostly in the fields of Electrical and Electronics engineering, with a special emphasis on helping the society. We are the student branch of Jadavpur University.

Who we are?

The Jadavpur University IEEE student branch, founded in 2010, belongs to the Kolkata section of Region 10 of the organization. We are a group of enthusiastic students who are promoting innovative ideas both within and outside the campus.

Our Activities

The Jadavpur University IEEE student branch conducts various events like seminars, workshops, paper presentations and tech quizzes. It is a platform where students come together, work, cooperate, coordinate, compete and as a result, develop both their intellectual as well as professional skills. Our events also involve welfare activities which have an impact on the society.

Our Vision

It is the vision of the Jadavpur University IEEE student branch to create an atmosphere which promotes research and innovative ideas. It is our vision to create a platform where students would get a chance to interact with researchers and industrial people in their respective fields of their interest. Our welfare activities, too, have a huge impact on the society at large.
