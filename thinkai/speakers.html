About Our Speakers

Anuj Duggal

Intel Corporation

Google launchpad

Currently working in Corporate Affairs Group in Intel, Anuj has also worked with Google on Google Launchpad Program to empower start-ups. A leading figure in the field of Machine Learning in India, he has shared his skills in the form of workshops, codelabs and tech talks, along with running Student Yatra-a unique initiative to enable students across India to start building with latest technologies at an early stage, thus inspiring the next-gen technology innovators.

Shivam Abhilash

ZS Associates

Vice Chair, IEEE CS India

Shivam is currently working in the Big Data team of ZS associates. An expert in the field of Machine Learning, he has worked on a large number of technical and professional projects. He has given talks as Guest Speaker in more than 25 conferences on Machine Learning and its applications and the various projects that he has been working on.

Amit Konar

Professor, JU ETCE

Robotics and artificial intelligence

Dr. Amit Konar, a professor in the Department of Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering, Jadavpur University, Kolkata received his Ph.D in Artificial Intelligence from Jadavpur University itself in 1994.In 1995, he joined as a professor there in the same department and has been teaching the subject of Artificial Intelligence to grad-students till now. Prof. Konar is a recipient of the 1997 Young Scientist Award, offered by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) for his significant contributions in Artificial Intelligence and Soft Computing. He is internationally acclaimed for his master works in the fields of Artificial Intelligence, Computational Neuroscience, Cognitive Robotics, Embedded Systems.
